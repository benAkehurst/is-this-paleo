What is this site?

"Is this Paleo" is a quick and easy way to check if a food is paleo friendly or not.
We put this site together to allow people a quick and easy way to check if food items are paleo friendly.

OK, so what is Paleo?

Put simply, Paleo refers to the Paleo diet.
Basically, its how human beings used to eat 10,000 years ago before we settled in cities.
It is now seen as a great diet plan to loose weight and get healty, as our bodies are better addapted to process paleo foods.
